Title: CameraComponent

Brief Description: This code snippet shows you how to manipulate the camera of your device. With this sample you can:

	* Select either the front facing camera or the back facing camera.
	* Select whether to turn the flash on, off or leave it in automatic mode.
	* Select the different resolution configurations of your camera.


Language: Object Pascal

Platforms supported: iOS, Android, Windows, OSX

Keywords: camera, flash, front, back, facing, iOS, Android, Windows, OSX, MAC, capture, video, resolution, frame rate
